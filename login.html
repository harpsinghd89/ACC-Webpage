Accessibility Statement

Accessibility Compliance Canada Inc. · Last reviewed: March 2026

Our Commitment

We are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities and continually improve the experience for all users.

Conformance

✅WCAG 2.1 Level AA — Exceeds the current AODA minimum of WCAG 2.0 AA.
✅Keyboard Navigation — All interactive elements keyboard accessible; skip-to-content link provided.
✅Screen Reader Support — Semantic HTML, ARIA roles, labels, and live regions throughout.
✅Touch Targets — All interactive elements meet the 44×44px minimum touch target.
✅Reduced Motion — Animations suppressed with prefers-reduced-motion.
✅Accessible Forms — Labels, aria-describedby errors, role="alert" validation.
✅Color Contrast — All text meets WCAG 2.1 AA minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio against all backgrounds.

Overview

We are committed to protecting personal information in compliance with CASL and Canadian privacy law.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact form: Name, company, size, email, and message.
  • Newsletter: Email address and express consent timestamp.
  • Technical: IP address, browser type, pages visited. No advertising trackers.

2. How We Use It

  • To respond to inquiries and provide services.
  • To send our newsletter only with express consent.
  • To improve our website and meet legal obligations.

We only send commercial emails with express consent. We record: email, date/time, method, and IP per subscriber, stored in Zoho CRM with CASL compliance mode enabled. Withdraw consent anytime via unsubscribe link or by emailing [email protected].

4. Security

5. Your Rights (PIPEDA)

6. Retention

Contact data: up to 3 years. Consent records: subscription duration + 3 years (CASL).
