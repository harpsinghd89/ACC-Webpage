Accessibility Statement

Our Commitment

We are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities and continually improve the experience for all users.

Conformance

✅WCAG 2.1 Level AA — Exceeds the current AODA minimum of WCAG 2.0 AA.
✅Keyboard Navigation — All interactive elements keyboard accessible; skip-to-content link provided.
✅Screen Reader Support — Semantic HTML, ARIA roles, labels, and live regions throughout.
✅Touch Targets — All interactive elements meet the 44×44px minimum touch target.
✅Reduced Motion — Animations suppressed with prefers-reduced-motion.
✅Accessible Forms — Labels, aria-describedby errors, role="alert" validation.
✅Color Contrast — All text meets WCAG 2.1 AA minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio against all backgrounds.
