ACC Widget Test Page

This is a minimal test page to verify the ACC Accessibility Widget loads and functions correctly in the production environment.

Test Checklist

  • FAB button appears in the bottom-right corner
  • Clicking FAB opens the accessibility panel
  • Font size, contrast, and spacing controls work
  • Language picker (globe icon) opens and translates
  • Restoring English reloads and reopens the panel
  • Panel closes cleanly
